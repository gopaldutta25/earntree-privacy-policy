Delete Your EarnTree Account

If you want to delete your account and all associated data, please follow these steps:

  1. Email us at earntreesupp@gmail.com from the email registered with your account.
  2. Include your username and request to delete your account.

Data that will be deleted:

  • Profile information (name, email, phone if provided)
  • Reward and referral data
  • Task and quiz history

Data retention note: Some anonymized analytics data may be retained for up to 90 days for reporting purposes.

Once processed, your account will be permanently removed from EarnTree.
